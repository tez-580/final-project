INTRODUCTION

COVID-19 has emerged as a pandemic with serious clinical manifestations including death. While tremendous efforts at the frontline are placed on detecting the virus, providing treatments, and developing vaccines, it is also critically important to examine the technologies and systems for tackling disease emergence and especially the strategy for disease prevention. The most spreading of this pandemic is due to the direct contact with an infected patient.

At this time we are introducing a robotic medical assistant called "COVID-19 PATIENT AID".The robots can be deployed in isolation wards for COVID-19 infected cases for delivery of food and medicine to patients, Monitoring health, and collection of contagious waste . The robot distributes medicines and foods to COVID-19 patients inside a COVID-19 ward and disinfects themselves before returning to a health worker who stays in a green zone. The health worker can control the robot from a safe distance and see real-time data on the PATIENT AID'S WEBSITE . The robot is equipped with multi-sensors With a large-capacity tray, the bot can deliver lots of medicines, meals, and other supplies to patients in the hospital to reduce the burden on the medical staff. An advantage of it is that the health workers can avoid going to red zones wearing PPE kits in a Covid treatment centre. After serving the patients foods and medicine, the robot can be disinfected inside a tend through a fogging method

WHY PATIENT AID?

The most spreading of this pandemic is due to the direct contact with an infected patient. The virus that causes covid 19 is mainly transmitted through droplets generated when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or exhales. Using our robot, the direct contact of medical practitioners and covid 19 isolated patients can be neglected. The major features of this device are temperature monitoring of patients, delivering drugs to the patients, providing sanitizer, and also health care workers can see patient pulse rate and temperature in our website conference. a large number of hospitals are seeking the help of Robotics out of an urgent need for non-contact delivery and health monitoring of covid patients. they can reduce contact between people and effectively prevents the spread of the virus. and also reduce the cost of purchasing PPE kits.
